Resource Generating Buildings

Movement: Up Down Left Right keys or W S A D keys
Create a building: Press B to enter Build mode. Press SHIFT-B to cycle through all buildings to create. Click a tile to create that building.
Create a unit: Open the Buildings sidebar menu, and click the View button for the barracks you want to create a unit for. Choose the unit from the dropdown and click Create Unit. The unit will appear after its given creation period.
Unit information: Hover over a unit and press the I key. Press the I key again over the unit to hide the unit information.
Unit attacking: Select a unit by clicking on it. Then hold down SHIFT and click another unit or building. The unit will start attacking the selected item. To view the health of a unit or building, select it; the selection ring will change from blue (full health), to green, to orange, to red (low health).
Hiding the sidebar: Press the H key to hide the sidebar when it is open.
